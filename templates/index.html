🔧 Pipeline

How It Works

Four deterministic steps from raw email to classification decision.

1
📥
Preprocessing
URL removal, email stripping, phone number cleaning, special character removal and whitespace normalization.
2
🔢
TF-IDF Vectorization
Convert text to 5,000 feature vectors using term frequency–inverse document frequency with 1–2 n-grams.
3
🌲
Random Forest Classification
An ensemble of 100 decision trees votes on the TF-IDF vector to produce a confident prediction.
4
✅
Final Decision
Spam or Legitimate verdict returned with confidence score in sub-millisecond latency.
