Architecture

Why TF-IDF + Random Forest?

The optimal balance between accuracy, speed, and resource efficiency for real-world deployment.

⚡
Real-Time Speed
Instant classification with sub-millisecond inference. Processes emails as fast as they arrive without queuing or batching overhead.
TF-IDF + RF: <1ms  vs  LSTM: ~50ms per email
🖥️
Lower Resource Usage
Runs efficiently on commodity hardware. No specialized GPU infrastructure required, dramatically reducing operational costs.
4 GB RAM  vs  8 GB for Deep Learning
🎯
High Accuracy
Achieves 96.31% classification accuracy on a diverse dataset of 193,850 emails — competitive with much heavier deep learning models.
96.31% on 193,850 emails · FPR 6.06%
🔧
No GPU Required
Entire training and inference pipeline runs on standard CPU hardware. Deployable anywhere — edge servers, cloud VMs, or on-premises.
Standard CPU · scikit-learn · No CUDA
